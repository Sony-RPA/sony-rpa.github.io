Scoring Rubric

75%+ (Ideal)

A score in this range is ideal for automation. Work that is repetitive happens to be the easiest to streamline.

50% - 75% (Fair)

These are tasks that require limited decision-making. We program our bots to account for all forseeable scenarios.

Under 50% (Hard)

Tasks which require input from 3rd parties are the hardest to maintain. But rest assured, we will find a way.

By the numbers
